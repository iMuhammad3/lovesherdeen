back

My online friend is Sophie Olamide Jegede. She has one of the weirdest name combinations ever. Who even names their child Jegede? Sophie is Nigerian, Irish and Scottish. I’m quite jealous. Her mom is Nigerian, her dad is Irish, and she has lived in Scotland for the amount years the country requires for you to be citizen. She was Muslim revert then and I remember teaching her somethings in Islam but now I don’t think she follows any religion. Why do I know so much about Sophie? Because we go way back.
It started during the lockdown in 2020. Everybody and their brother were on this app called House Party. An app where you can create a party with your friends and just video chat while playing games or doing some other activities. You could also join random parties that were set to public. The app discontinued in 2022 due to some issues they had with some big company I can’t remember, maybe Apple or something. If you haven’t guessed already, I met Sophie on the app, through my uncle who is 3 years older than me. In my head I used to call him a “pedo” for talking to random girls that were 5 years younger than him. 17 year old talking to 12 year olds? C’mon Uncle Muhammad you’re better than that.
We’d be on house party with my friends and Sophie and her friends and also random people every night. Lockdown was a really cruel but fun time. Then eventually Sophie and I were chatting everyday and somehow I became besties with a random stranger that lived in a country which is also inside a country. The Brits are some really confused lads if I’m being honest. I don’t remember what exactly made us besties or what we used to talk about everyday but it happened and few years later she came to Nigeria.
In June of 2023, Sophie chatted me up having not spoken for 1 year plus cause there wasn’t really anything to talk about anymore. After all there was no more lockdown so we had school to go back to and lives to live. She texted me like it was just to catch up and I replied. Then 2 days later she sent a snap with location showing she was in Nigeria. First thing that came to my mind was “we have to meet!”, and we did. She was in some state in the southern Nigeria, either Ondo, Oyo, Ogun, or Osun. I really can’t tell the difference between those states. She was in Lagos as well before she finally came to Abuja for 4 days. Funny thing is we almost met all 4 days.
First day of her being in Abuja we decided I’d come and meet her at her uncles house where she was staying, which was just few minutes from my own house. The reason I didn’t end up going was because I already made plans to play football with my friends who actually also know her. I didn’t want to abandon my friends for Sophie mostly because it didn’t feel right and besides we had 3 more days to meet.
On the second day she said she was following her mom to Jabi Lake Mall to some dentist or eye clinic, I can’t remember. I told her I’d come and we could buy fan ice from Shoprite. She used to talk about how she liked fan ice back in 2020. So I drove to Jabi Lake Mall, 3 minute walk from my house by the way. I entered the building and suddenly I was scared to see her. I didn’t know if she was taller than me, I didn’t know what i was going to say even though at that point we’d spoken for thousands of hours online over 2 years. I wanted to run away, but I convinced myself not to, after all I was trying to become less of an introvert then. She texted that she was at the entrance of Shoprite and there I went and saw her together with her Mom’s boyfriend. I greeted him and he was very friendly and told us to have a nice day. And there was Sophie, shorter than me but looked the same as she did in pictures and snaps. Thank God I didn’t get catfished. Before we met I texted her not to attempt to hug me, so I didn’t worry about whether she was going to hug me or not. The first thing she told me was that she needed to pee, so i escorted her to the restroom and held her bag before she was done. We entered Shoprite and picked up some fan ice. I was ready to pay the bills until she picked some Oreo thing in the freezer for 8k?! I let her take lead and she paid for everything. While we were in Shoprite, two guys approached us and asked if we were dating, we didn’t answer but we stared at them with a face that said, the f***? Then one of them asked Sophie for her number. She gave him a wrong number and we left the place laughing. We passed by KFC and she said let’s eat. I’m like okay rich girl let’s eat. We ordered some food, chatted as we waited, sent snaps to our mutual friends and ate. Few minutes later her mom came to us and I greeted and that was the end of our meeting and I’d never see her again… sike!
We met the next day. Yeah we decided we wanted more so she somehow found Blu Cabana on google maps and said let’s go there. I had never gone to Blu Cabana but I knew it was one of them fancy rich people restaurants. We were set to meet at 4pm. Her uncle drove her and they arrived at 3:58pm waiting for me. I was still at home praying Asr. I was done praying and quickly left and arrived at 4:08pm. I greeted the uncle but he didn’t seem too friendly, after he left she told me he said I’m doing African time. Like bro i was literally 8 minutes late! African time is 2 hours after event time. But anyways we entered and she led the way, asking about the place and getting us a table, as if it wasn’t my country she came to. It might be worth noting that this outing I actually ditched my friends who asked me to go play football with them. Anyways we checked out their menu and not to my surprise she wanted to get everything on the menu. I, on the other had couldn’t tell what was what cause all I saw was some weird fancy food names that I didn’t have a clue what they were. So I ordered fruits and pizza. I also couldn’t tell what drinks had alcohol in them or not, I was even thinking of buying water. To be honest I can’t remember what type of drink I bought. We talked about a bunch of things that day. I can’t remember anything else. We were done and she took a cute video of us while we waited for her uncle. That was it… or was it?
On her final day we wanted to meet again at her uncle’s house. She said after she was done at the salon then I could come. She spent way more time there than we expected so we forgot about it. And that was it, she was done with her summer vacay in Nigeria and had flown back to her country. Maybe one I’ll go to Scotland and meet her to buy her KFC.
